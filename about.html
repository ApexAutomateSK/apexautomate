About ApexAutomate

Modern workforce planning made smarter.

ApexAutomate connects seamlessly with your HR tech stack — including Workday, Greenhouse, ADP, SAP, and Excel — to eliminate the manual handoffs that slow teams down. By automating repetitive HR tasks and syncing your data in real time, we help your team move from disconnected systems & spreadsheets to intelligent, always-on workflows.

We combine smart automation with deep HR expertise — delivering tools that simplify complexity without sacrificing power. Whether you're building a headcount plan, analyzing turnover, or syncing data across systems, ApexAutomate gives your teams what they need to move faster and smarter.

At our core, we believe HR data automation should be a strategic advantage — not a maintenance burden. That’s why ApexAutomate is built to reduce friction, enhance visibility, and bring clarity to your people operations.

Whether you're scaling, restructuring, or aligning talent to business goals, ApexAutomate is your automation layer — built to unlock cleaner data, faster planning, and confident decisions.
