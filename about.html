About ApexAutomate

At ApexAutomate, we’re focused on transforming how organizations plan, scale, and optimize their workforce using the power of AI. Founded by a team of experts in people analytics, HR strategy, and intelligent automation, ApexAutomate exists to simplify workforce planning for modern, fast-moving businesses.

Our platform connects seamlessly with leading HR systems like Workday, Greenhouse, ADP, and SAP — helping teams move away from disconnected spreadsheets and toward dynamic, AI-assisted modeling. Whether you're a scaling startup or an established enterprise, ApexAutomate provides scenario planning, headcount forecasting, and real-time analytics to help you stay ahead of business demands.

We combine technical innovation with a deep understanding of organizational complexity — offering tools that are both powerful and easy to use. From AI-powered attrition modeling to executive-ready dashboards, we help HR, finance, and operations leaders make smarter decisions, faster.

At our core, we believe workforce planning should be a strategic advantage — not a reporting headache. That’s why we’re building tools that reduce friction, increase clarity, and empower your team to act with confidence.

Whether you're preparing for growth, reducing risk, or aligning hiring with strategic goals — ApexAutomate is your partner in workforce planning that works.
